FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most likely questions about the rebuilt site, deployment options, and form delivery architecture.

Can this be hosted on GitHub Pages?

Yes. The frontend is plain static HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Form submission should point to an external endpoint such as the included Cloudflare Worker.

Can we use Resend?

Yes. The included worker is designed for Resend because it removes SMTP timeout and Gmail OAuth issues. You will need a verified sending domain in Resend.

What is the free workaround?

Cloudflare Workers has a generous free tier and pairs well with a static site. For email delivery itself, most providers still require some verified sender setup.
