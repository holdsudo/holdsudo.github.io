Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have questions? We have answers.
Browse the answers to common questions about financing below.

Who is Champion Auto Finance?

Champion Auto Finance is an independent auto finance company service provider that specializes in helping consumers and independent dealerships secure competitive vehicle financing. We focus on transparency, compliance, and matching each client with the right lending solution without rate packing or hidden markups.

Why Choose Champion Auto Finance?

Champion Auto Finance operates differently than traditional dealerships and finance offices. Our model is built on transparency, ethical lending practices, and long-term relationships. Clients are presented with true lender buy rates, clear disclosures, and straightforward financing options with no pressure and no hidden tactics.

Unlike traditional dealerships or banks that may mark up interest rates, Champion Auto Finance does NOT engage in rate packing meaning clients receive lender-approved buy rates, and our compensation is not tied to increasing interest rates, ensuring an honest and consumer-first financing experience.

I’m an independent used car dealer without a bank relationship. Can you help?

Yes. Champion Auto Finance partners with independent dealers who lack direct lender relationships. We act as your financing partner by submitting deals, coordinating approvals, and helping you close sales while staying compliant with state and federal regulations.

I want to buy out my lease and finance it. Can you help?

Absolutely. We assist consumers with lease buyouts by arranging financing directly, even if the original dealer is not involved. We help navigate payoff amounts, lender approvals, and documentation to make the process smooth and transparent.

Can you help me finance a vehicle purchased from a private seller or marketplace?

Yes. Champion Auto Finance can help arrange financing for vehicles purchased through private sellers, online marketplaces, or non traditional sources, subject to lender guidelines and vehicle eligibility.

What is the financing process?

The process begins with a secure application and document review. From there, we match your profile with the most appropriate lenders, present clear financing options, and guide you through approval, documentation, and funding. You’re kept informed at every step. Simply click Get Approved, and we’ll take it from there.

Is my credit information secure?

Yes. Protecting your personal and financial information is a top priority. We use secure systems and follow strict data protection standards to ensure your information is handled responsibly and confidentially.

Will applying hurt my credit?

In many cases, lenders use soft or limited credit inquiries during the initial review process. If a full credit pull is required, we will disclose this upfront so there are no surprises.

What types of credit profiles do you work with?

We work with a wide range of credit profiles, including prime, near-prime, and sub-prime borrowers. Each application is evaluated individually based on lender criteria.

Do you charge hidden fees?

No. Champion Auto Finance is committed to transparency. Any applicable fees are clearly disclosed upfront in compliance with state and federal regulations.

Are you licensed and compliant?

Yes. Champion Auto Finance operates in accordance with New Jersey Department of Banking and Insurance (NJDOBI) requirements and applicable consumer finance laws.

Disclaimer: Champion Auto Finance is not a lender. Financing approvals, terms, and rates are subject to lender underwriting, credit approval, and applicable laws. Licensing and availability may vary by state.
