Why Choose Us

Champion Auto Finance was founded with a clear mission: to deliver a more transparent, ethical, and client focused approach to auto financing. With over 20 years of hands on experience in the finance industry, our leadership brings deep knowledge, proven judgment, and a commitment to doing business the right way. We understand that securing vehicle financing can often feel confusing, stressful, or unclear. Too often, consumers are left questioning whether the numbers they are presented truly reflect their best approval. Champion Auto Finance was created specifically to change that experience.

  • Transparency Without Rate Packing At Champion Auto Finance, we do not engage in rate packing. That means we do not inflate interest rates to increase compensation. Instead, our clients receive the true buy rate provided by our lending partners clearly explained and fully disclosed. Our compensation structure is based on flat rate approvals determined by the lending tier and approval profile, not by increasing a customer’s interest rate. This approach ensures that our interests are aligned with those of our clients, fostering trust, clarity, and long term relationships.
  • A Professional, Client First Approach We work with a broad network of banks and lending institutions across multiple credit tiers, allowing us to structure financing solutions that are tailored, responsible, and competitive. Every application is reviewed with care, discretion, and respect for our clients’ financial goals. Whether working directly with consumers or alongside dealerships, Champion Auto Finance operates with strict compliance standards, ethical guidelines, and a commitment to accuracy at every step of the process.
  • A Better Way to Finance Champion Auto Finance stands for integrity, transparency, and expertise. Our goal is not simply to arrange financing but to build confidence, provide clarity, and deliver a better financing experience from start to finish.
