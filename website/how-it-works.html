How It Works

Champion Auto Finance positions itself as a transparent financing partner with true rate approvals and no rate packing.

1

Apply

Prospects or dealer partners submit an inquiry through a simple static form that posts to a secure backend endpoint.

2

Review

Your team receives the lead directly by email through Resend or another provider without relying on WordPress mail plugins.

3

Respond

Follow up with approved terms, lender options, and next steps through your normal sales or operations workflow.
