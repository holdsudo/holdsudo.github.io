How It Works

Champion Auto Finance provides transparent, compliant auto financing solutions for both dealerships and individual consumers. We act as the financing specialist—connecting applicants with qualified lenders while maintaining clarity, speed,  and ethical standards throughout the process.

For Dealership Partners

Who This Is For:
Independent dealerships or sellers who prefer to outsource financing or do not maintain a direct bank.

Get in touch

Process

1. Dealer submits customer application and vehicle details.
2. Credit, income, and deal structure are reviewed.
3. Application is matched with our lending network.
4. True buy rate and approval terms are disclosed.
5. Dealer completes the sale while we coordinate funding.

Why Dealers Partner With Us

• One point of access to multiple lenders
* No need to maintain direct bank relationships
• Flat rate, transparent approvals
• Faster approvals and professional structuring
• Full compliance and documentation support

For Individual Customers

Who This Is For:
Consumers financing a lease buyout, private party purchase, or marketplace vehicle.

Get in touch

Process

1. Consumer completes a secure application.
2. Credit, income, and vehicle details are reviewed.
3. Application is submitted to qualified lenders.
4. Approved terms and true lender buy rate are clearly presented.
5. We coordinate directly with the seller or title holder to fund the transaction.

What Sets Us Apart

• No rate packing — true lender buy rates only
• No hidden fees or surprises
• Financing solutions across multiple credit tiers
• Professional guidance from application to funding
